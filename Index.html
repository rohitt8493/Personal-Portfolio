An academic assistant chatbot tailored for students that answers curriculum-related questions, explains concepts, and provides references. The bot is designed to give accurate, concise answers and suggest study resources while clearly indicating when it is unsure.

Summary: The chatbot uses retrieval-augmented responses and curated academic content to improve accuracy. It includes fallback messages and encourages users to verify critical answers with instructors or textbooks.
